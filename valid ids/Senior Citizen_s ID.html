Senior Citizen ID

The OSCA (Office of Senior Citizen Affairs) ID, also known as Senior Citizen ID, is a valid identification card with no expiration for Filipinos aged 60 years and above. Seniors with dual citizenship are also eligible to apply by providing proof of Filipino citizenship and at least six months of residency in the Philippines. Holders may avail of healthcare privileges, transportation benefits, utility discounts, and taxation. However, these privileges can still be enjoyed without the ID by presenting any government-issued ID.

REQUIREMENTS

  • Birth certificate
  • 1X1 ID picture
  • Valid government-issued ID

    • RENEWAL

  • Accomplished application form
  • Valid ID or a Barangay Certificate of Residency
  • Two (2) copies of 1x1 photo
  • Affidavit of Loss

PROCEDURE

  1. Visit the nearest Office for Senior Citizens Affairs (OSCA) in your area in Davao City.
  2. Upon submission of requirements, prepare more supporting documents that will verify the applicant's eligibility for application. This may be a photocopy of your address proof, other government-issued IDs, and a Marriage Certificate/ Contract, depending on the documents specified by the local jurisdiction.
  3. Applicants may apply through walk-ins or online. For walk-ins, request an application and fill out the necessary details:
    • District
    • Barangay
    • Complete name
    • Age
    • Sex
    • Civil status
    • Date of Birth
    • Details of your family
    • Signature
  4. Submit the requirements and wait for the card to be issued.
  5. For online registration, visit the official site of the OSCAand select your District.
  6. Supply the required information, which includes:
    • Full Name
    • Address
    • Place of Birth
    • Date of Birth
    • Age
    • Contact Number
    • Gender
    • Civil Status
    • Contact Person
    • Emergency Contact Number
    • Name of Senior Association (required)
    • Applicant's Email Address
  7. Upload the following file requirements and click Submit Application:
    • 2 Pieces 1"x1" ID Picture with collar
    • Proof of Age, any of the following:
      • Birth Certificate
      • Baptismal Certificate
      • Government Issued ID with Birthdate and Mailing Address
      • Proof of Residency: Barangay Certificate with Inclusion of Date of Birth
      • For Replacement: 2 Pieces 1"x1" ID Picture with collar/ Barangay Certificate (if applicable)
    • Check your email address for further instructions. Note that the processing time is within five (5) business days.

RENEWAL

Dependent beneficiaries from PhilHealth members, self-employed, and currently employed citizens who needs to update their coverage are eligible for renewal of PhilHealth membership.

  1. Visit the nearest PhilHealth office.
  2. Fill out a renewal form.
  3. Attach and submit the required documents to the PhilHealth officer.
  4. Pay for the renewal fee, if applicable.
  5. A receipt of renewed membership will be given as a proof of transaction.
