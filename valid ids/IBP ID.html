Integrated Bar of the Philippines (IBP) Identification Card (ID)

The Integrated Bar of the Philippines (IBP) ID is issued for the citizens who are officially a member of legal profession in the country. The passers of the Philippine Bar Examination administered by the Supreme Court are only eligible to be a holder. This identity card has two types, the national and the local IBP card. With its two (2) year validity, holders are given the permission to give legal advice, practice law, and notarize documents.

REQUIREMENTS

  • Duly-accomplished form
  • Two (2) 1x1 picture
  • Three (3) Specimen of your signature

PROCEDURE

    LOCAL IBP CARD

  1. Visit the nearest IBP Office of the Chapter in Davao City.
  2. Fill out the form similar form provided during the clearance. Provide the following details:
    • IB Chapter
    • Roll number
    • Lifetime member number
    • Full name
    • Date of Birth
    • Residence Address
    • Office Address
    • Contact information
    • Occupation
    • Field of Specialization
    • Law School
    • Email Address
    • Year admitted to bar
    • Photo
    • Signature
    • Payment details
  3. Submit the requirements to the printing press accredited by the Davao IBP Chapter.
  4. Wait for seven or fourteen business days to receive the IBP ID.
