Government Service Insurance System (GSIS) UMID ID

The Unified Multi-Purpose Identification (UMID) card is exclusive to members of the Government Service Insurance System (GSIS) and the Social Security System (SSS) in the Philippines. It has a life span of 10 years. It is used to transact for Development Mutual Fund (Pag-IBIG Fund or HDMF), the Philippine Health Insurance Corporation (PhilHealth), the Government Service Insurance System (GSIS), and the Social Security System (SSS). It can also serve as an ATM card, Identification card, and Debit card. Filipino citizens who are active members and GSIS Pensioners can apply for the GSIS UMID card.

REQUIREMENTS

  • UMID CARD Application Form
  • A copy of any government-issued valid ID

PROCEDURE

  1. Visit the nearest GSIS Branch.
  2. Fill out the application form.
  3. Submit the necessary documents.
  4. Secure your UMID eCard release form and capture your biometrics.
  5. If you are a first-time applicant, pay for the processing fee.
  6. A pickup schedule will be sent to the applicant through SMS from the GSIS. Prepare two (2) valid government-issued IDs to claim your cards.

    For pensioners, in person or through an Authorized Representative the requirements to bring: Signed Authorization Letter OR notarized Special Power of Attorney (SPA);

    • A copy of a pensioner holding the Authorization Letter or SPA;
    • Photocopy of the pensioner’s two (2) valid government-issued IDs and their originals
    • Photocopy of the authorized representative’s two (2) valid government-issued IDs and their originals.
  7. Once claimed, activate your UMID card on your nearest GSIS Wireless Activated Processing System kiosk.

RENEWAL

  1. Fill out the UMID Card replacement form.
  2. Submit your accomplished replacement form.
  3. Pay for the replacement fee.
  4. Patiently wait for a notice by email or text regarding the schedule of claiming the card.
