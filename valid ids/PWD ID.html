Persons with Disabilities (PWD) ID

The PWD ID is an identification card valid for three (3) years issued to any bona fide Filipino of any age with disabilities associated with psychosocial disability resulting from a chronic illness, learning, visual, orthopaedic, mental, hearing, and speech impairment. Holders are entitled to a twenty (20) discount and Value Added Tax (VAT) exemption, a five (5) per cent discount on necessities and prime commodities, and a membership for the Social Security System (SS) or Government Service Insurance System (GSIS), PhilHealth, and Pag-IBIG. In addition, educational assistance and priority lanes are available for PWDs.

REQUIREMENTS

  • Two (2) recent 1x1 ID photos with name and signature or thumb marks at the back
  • One (1) valid ID
  • Any valid document that certifies the applicant as disabled, this may be in the form of the latest school assessment/medical certificate/or disability certificate

PROCEDURE

  1. Accomplish the PWD ID application form; you may request a form online or manually on the offices in Davao City. Note: You must still visit the Municipal/City Social Welfare Development Office to comply with the requirements and PWD ID.
  2. Submit your requirements on your nearest or preferred site.
  3. The office will assess your application and verify the information provided.
  4. Once approved, the applicant may claim their PWD ID.

RENEWAL

There are two ways to renew your PWD ID, which may be done in person or online.

  1. For online renewal, the applicant may visit the Service Request of Department of Health(DOH).
  2. For walk-ins, the applicant may visit the nearest National Council on Disability Affairs in Davao City.
