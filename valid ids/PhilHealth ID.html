PhilHealth ID

The PhilHealth ID is a valid identification card from the Philippine Health Insurance Corporation (PHIC) that seeks to assist Filipino citizens in healthcare. It has no expiry membership for at least 21-year-old Filipinos who are not allowed as dependents. Holders are eligible for PhilHealth benefits and privileges by the government's insurance program.

REQUIREMENTS

  • Two (2) signed copies of the PhilHealth Member Registration Form (PMRF)
  • Two (2) latest 1 x 1 ID pictures
  • Two (2) valid ID documents
  • A copy of a PSA-issued birth certificate or Marriage Certificate for the married-female applicant.

PROCEDURE

  1. Regular PhilHealth ID
    1. Visit your nearest PhilHealth office and complete the request form for a PhilHealth Identification Card (PIC).
    2. Submit the requirements.
    3. When the Member’s Data Record (MDR) and PhilHealth are processed, double-check the information and claim your ID.
  2. Digitized PhilHealth Insurance ID Card
  3. The digitized PhilHealth ID is only available for employed members, and they must coordinate with their HR office to order in bulk.

RENEWAL

Dependent beneficiaries from PhilHealth members, self-employed, and currently employed citizens who needs to update their coverage are eligible for renewal of PhilHealth membership.

  1. Visit the nearest PhilHealth office.
  2. Fill out a renewal form.
  3. Attach and submit the required documents to the PhilHealth officer.
  4. Pay for the renewal fee, if applicable.
  5. A receipt of renewed membership will be given as a proof of transaction.
